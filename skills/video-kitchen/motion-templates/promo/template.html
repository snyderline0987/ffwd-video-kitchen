🔌 The Workflow

From raw ingredients to Michelin-star video in three easy steps

📥

1. Plug In

docker-compose up -d starts the agent + dashboard. Your footage stays local — no uploads, no cloud processing.

🤖

2. Agent Cooks

Tell the agent what you want: "Make a 30s news overview from this article." It picks the recipe, applies film theory, generates the composition.

🍽️

3. Serve Video

Preview, tweak, render — all locally. Broadcast-ready output in minutes, not hours.

📜 Kitchen Rules

The secret sauce: Four cooking methods for cinematic excellence

🔥

Grill

Edward Dmytryk's Seven Rules

Quick cuts, clear continuity, precise timing. Perfect for news and promo content that needs to land hard.

💥

Flambé

Eisenstein's Montage Theory

Collision of ideas, emotional shock, dramatic juxtaposition. For when you need to make a statement.

🍲

Slow Cook

Menke / Tarantino Style

Dialogue-driven, tension building, timing is everything. The art of the hold and the perfect punchline.

🥘

Sous Vide

Murch's Rule of Six

Emotion first, precision temperature control. Every cut serves the feeling, not the timeline.
